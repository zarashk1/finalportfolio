FOR MENTEES

  1. He found art through appendicitis.

    He suffered an attack of appendicitis at age twenty. His mother brought him art supplies to help him pass time; it was then he discovered his love for art.

  2. He was good friends with Pablo Picasso.

    Matisse playfully referred to their friendship as a "boxing match": they were lifelong friends and major artistic rivals.

  3. He ran an art school for a couple years.

    He ran the Academie Matisse in Paris from 1908 to 1911. He had his pupils draw copies of casts, sent them to the Lourve on weekends, and discussed color theory.

  4. He is considered a founder of Favuism.

    Fauvism reinterpreted color as a means of expression. Matisse famously once said "When I put down a green, it doesn‘t mean grass; and when I put down a blue, it doesn't mean the sky"

  5. He studied law.

    He passed his bar exam with distinction and was a practicing law clerk.

  6. He had three cats.

    Matisse's cats were named Minouche, Coussi, and La Puce and he fed them pieces of brioche every morning.
